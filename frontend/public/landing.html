The Problem

Innovation intake is fragmented and slow

Business stakeholders submit free-text pitches, which are often lost or duplicated. IPM auto-classifies them with NLP (objective, impact, category, context), detects duplicates, and routes the initiative through a user-driven pipeline across three phases: Sourcing -> Qualification -> Delivery.

The Solution

IPM: The 3-Phase Innovation Pipeline

A structured workflow with 3 Stage Gates ensures only the best ideas move forward. Sourcing (Discovery) -> Qualification (Evaluation) -> Delivery (Selection). Every stage is supported by AI to accelerate decision making.

How We Work

IPM Flow blends intuitive submission, AI-powered classification, and rigorous stage gates into a continuous innovation pipeline. The platform analyzes your pitch, maps it to the right horizon, and coordinates Discovery, Evaluation, and Selection.
